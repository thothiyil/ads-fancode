Live sport is the last appointment medium India has left. Fans don't time-shift it, they don't scroll past it, and on the big nights they put the rest of their lives on hold for it. That is not a media buy. That is a captive audience showing up on a clock you can plan around.

FanCode reaches five million of those fans every month, across twelve sports — IPL, F1, La Liga, MotoGP, Pro Kabaddi, NFL, and a dozen more — through one product designed for the kind of fan who wants every ball, every lap, every set.

This issue is the brief: who they are, what we sell, and the formats that win when attention is a finite resource and the toss is twelve seconds away.
